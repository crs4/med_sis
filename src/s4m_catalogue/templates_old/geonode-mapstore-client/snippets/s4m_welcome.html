Welcome to the Soils4Med Soil Information System

The Soils Information System platform represents a significant collaborative achievement, developed under the framework of the PRIMA-funded Soils4Med project. This initiative brings together the expertise and resources of more than 15 leading academic institutions and research centers across the Mediterranean region.Through their combined efforts and diverse contributions, these organizations have created a comprehensive soil monitoring and information system that exemplifies the power of international scientific cooperation.
